Travel Tips For India

Essential travel tips for a smooth and enjoyable trip across India!

Packing Essentials

India has different climates in every region, so pack smartly. Carry light cotton clothes for summer destinations, warm jackets for mountain areas, comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, sunglasses, and a reusable water bottle for daily travel.

Budget Travel Tips

Travel affordably by using Indian Railways and public transport. Book hotels and tickets in advance to avoid high prices. Enjoy local street food and explore local markets for budget-friendly shopping.

Safety Tips

Always keep your important documents safe and avoid isolated areas at night. Use official taxis or ride services, stay alert in crowded places, and save emergency contacts on your phone while travelling.

Cultural Etiquette

Respect local traditions and customs while visiting religious places. Dress modestly inside temples, remove footwear before entering homes or sacred places, and greet people politely with “Namaste”.
